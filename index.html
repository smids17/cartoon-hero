A Guide To Avatar the Last Airbender

About Aang

Aang is a 112 year old airbender who was frozen in ice for 100 years. He is the avatar and while MIA the fire nation created an empire of conquered lands all over the wolrd. Aang must master the elements in order to defeat the fire lord and bring balance to the world once again.

Team Avatar

Team Avatar is the group of kids who travel with Aang and eventually help him defeat the fire lord

Katara is a waterbender from the southern water tribe. She discovered Aang with her brother and after freeing him from the ice, the trio traveled around the world taking part in thrilling adventures. Katara eventually becomes a waterbending master and teaches Aang.

Sokka is a warrior from the southern water tribe who, along with his sister Katara, rescued Aang from the ice. He is part of the original group of three (Aang, Katara, and Sokka) that traveled around the world helping Aang master all elements. Sokka ends up becoming a master swordsman and is a vital part in the final battle.

Toph is a blind earthbending master from one of the richest families in the entire Earth Kingdom. Because she is blind, her father thinks she is helpless and takes sheltering her to an extreme extent. Toph ends up running away from home and teaching Aang how to earthbend. She also develops a new type of bending: metal bending.

Zuko is the son of the Fire Lord and a master firebender. At a young age he is scarred by his cruel father and then exhiled. He hunted the avatar passionately in order to regain his honor by capturing Aang. Eventually, Zuko realized how evil the Fire Nation was and joined Team Avatar in order to defeat his father. Not only did he teach Aang firebending, but he also played a key role in the final battle.

Suki is the leader of the Kyoshi Warriors, an all female team of expert fighters. Though she is introduced earlier in the show, Suki ends up joining the group down the line after Sokka and Zuko break her out of a Fire Nation prison. Using fans as her primary weapon, she helps the group throughout their later adventures as well as in the final battle.
